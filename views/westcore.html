About Westcore Links

Westcore Links is a premier long-haul transport company based in Nisku, Alberta, serving all destinations across North America with a fleet of over 50 trucks. Our 24/7/365 schedule can accommodate both private and corporate projects, guaranteeing safe and timely hauls with over 60 years of industry excellence.

Our Services

Comprehensive transportation solutions tailored to your needs

🚛 Full Truckload (FTL)

Dedicated trucks for your entire shipment, ensuring direct delivery and maximum security for large freight across North America.

📦 Less Than Truckload (LTL)

Cost-effective solutions for smaller shipments, sharing truck space while maintaining the same professional standards and reliability.

⚖️ Oversized Loads

Specialized equipment and expert handling for heavy machinery, construction equipment, and oversized cargo requiring special permits.

⚡ Hot Shot Services

Expedited delivery for urgent shipments with flexible scheduling and rapid response times for time-sensitive freight.

Versatile hauling options for vehicles and equipment, with enclosed trailers available for weather protection and added security.

🛣️ Dedicated Lanes

Consistent, scheduled routes for regular shipments, providing predictable delivery times and dedicated capacity for your business.
