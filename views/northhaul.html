About NorthHaul

NorthHaul is the specialized bulk division of Westcore Links, providing dedicated food-grade, chemical, and freight transportation services across North America. With over 30 modern tankers and a 24/7/365 operation schedule, we deliver reliability, safety, and efficiency in every haul.

Our commitment to excellence and strict adherence to industry regulations ensures your cargo is transported with the highest standards of safety and professionalism.

Specialized Transportation Services

Expert handling for your most demanding cargo requirements

🥛 Food-Grade Transportation

Specialized Equipment:

  • FDA-compliant stainless steel tankers
  • Temperature-controlled systems
  • Dedicated food-grade fleet
  • Regular sanitation and inspection

Safe transportation of edible oils, dairy products, liquid sweeteners, and other food-grade materials with full FSMA compliance.

⚗️ Chemical Transportation

Certified Handling:

  • HAZMAT certified drivers
  • DOT/Transport Canada approved equipment
  • Emergency response procedures
  • Specialized containment systems

Expert transport of industrial chemicals, petroleum products, and hazardous materials with complete regulatory compliance and safety protocols.

📦 Bulk Freight Transportation

Versatile Solutions:

  • Cross-border expertise

Efficient bulk freight services with modern fleet, experienced drivers, and proven track record for timely deliveries across the continent.
