Our History

Westcore Links is a customer service driven trucking company based in Nisku, Alberta. We are a full-service LTL/Full Load Carrier and Hot Shot Carrier with over sixty years experience in the heavy hauling industry.

1960s

Foundation Years

Started operations serving the growing oil and gas industry in Alberta with a focus on reliability and safety.

1990s

Expansion Era

Expanded our fleet and services to cover routes across Canada and into the United States.

2020s

Modern Excellence

Grew to 50+ trucks and trailers with cutting-edge technology and 24/7/365 operations serving all of North America.
